ARMath

Augmented Reality Snapchat Lens


ARMath is an educational Snapchat lens that teaches children about shapes, colors, and mathematics. Using real-world markers, the lens presents children with simple math equations and a legend that maps shapes and colors to numbers.



Children have to determine which shape and color to reveal to the camera, and if they get it right, they are presented with the next question. The lens is fun, interactive, and a great way to learn about these subjects in an intuitive and engaging way.
